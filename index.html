Before the Big Day

Our Story

From a chance meeting at a friend's rooftop party to saying yes under the stars — here is how we found our way to forever.

March 2019

How We Met

Simon spilled his drink on Emma's shoes at a mutual friend's birthday. She laughed instead of getting mad. They talked until 2 AM about travel, old films, and the best ramen in the city.

He asked for her number. She said yes before he finished the sentence.

The Beginning
August 2021

Our First Adventure

A two-week road trip through the Italian countryside — wrong turns, vineyard lunches, and a sunset in Positano that made them both go quiet.

That was the moment they knew this was the person they'd choose, again and again.

Positano, Italy
June 2025

The Proposal

Simon planned a "casual dinner" at their favourite restaurant. After dessert, he got down on one knee in the garden out back — string lights, Emma's favourite song, and a ring he'd been hiding for three months.

She said yes. The whole restaurant cheered.

She Said Yes
What to Wear

Dress Code

Garden formal — think soft elegance in our wedding palette. We want the day to feel like a sun-dappled afternoon in an English garden.

For Our Guests

  • Style: Cocktail or garden-party formal
  • Colours: Sage green, ivory, dusty white, soft gold accents
  • Please avoid: Bright white (reserved for the bride), neon colours, and bold prints
  • Footwear: The ceremony is on grass — block heels or flats recommended
  • Layers: December evenings can be cool — bring a light wrap or jacket
